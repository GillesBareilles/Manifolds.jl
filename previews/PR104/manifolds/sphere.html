Base.exp ‚Äî Method
exp(M::Sphere, x, v)

Compute the exponential map from x into the tangent direction v on the Sphere M by following the great arc eminating from x in direction v along with length of v.

\[\exp_x v = \cos(\lVert v \rVert_x)x + \sin(\lVert v \rVert_x)\frac{v}{\lVert v \rVert_x},\]

where $\lVert v \rVert_x$ is the norm on the Sphere M.

Base.log ‚Äî Method
log(M::Sphere, x, y)

Compute the logarithmic map on the Sphere M, i.e. the tangent vector, whose geodesic starting from x reaches y after time 1. The formula reads for $x ‚â† -y$

\[\log_x y = d_{ùïä^n}(x,y) \frac{y-‚ü®x,y‚ü© x}{\lVert y-‚ü®x,y‚ü© x \rVert_2},\]

and a deterministic choice from the set of tangent vectors is returned if $x=-y$, i.e. for opposite points.
