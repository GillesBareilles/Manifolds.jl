    Array manifold

    Array manifold

    Manifolds.ArrayManifold — Type.
    ArrayManifold{M <: Manifold} <: Manifold

    A manifold to encapsulate manifolds working on array representations of MPoints and TVectors in a transparent way, such that for these manifolds its not necessary to introduce explicit types for the points and tangent vectors, but they are encapusalted/stripped automatically when needed.
